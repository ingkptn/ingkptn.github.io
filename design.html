core 2: interaction

A Memento of Home


A book for Core 2: Typography exploring my notions of home, from a physical place to things beyond that. The content ranges from childhood memories to traditional beliefs and values, and the simple design system reflects the style I am reminded of when thinking of the word "home".

Periodic Table Poster


Poster of the Periodic table of Elements emphasizing grid systems and typographic hierarchy. My design was made to resemble black-and-white photocopied worksheets we had back in middle school, and only one typeface was used.

Exhibition


Mock-ups of promotional material for an imagined exhibtiion for graffiti artist Alex Face, whose work surrounds a character of a child in a bunny costume. Both static and dynamic material were designed.
